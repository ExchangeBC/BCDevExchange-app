What is a Digital Resource?

Digital resources are software code, data and application programing interfaces (APIs).

What sort of resources does BCDevExchange list?

The BCDevExchange lists B.C. public sector digital resources licensed under permissive licenses, with an initial focus on code and APIs.

Why should I list my organization’s digital resources with the BCDevExchange?

If you want new ways to innovate and meet the needs of those you serve, then listing your digital resources makes sense. Why?

  • It makes your digital resources easier to find and use by developers and entrepreneurs so they can create services and innovations useful to those you serve.
  • It creates new ways to solve your business problems with faster IT development.
  • It connects you with experienced and skilled entrepreneurs and developers in B.C.’s tech and startup community.

How do you register your Resources?


Currently, there are two ways to list your digital resources on the BCDevExchange.

  • If you have a digital resource in a GitHub repo, send us an email or create an Issue in our GitHub Resources Repo , and we’ll work with you to get it listed on the BCDevExchange.
  • If you have digital resources in the BC Data Catalogue or elsewhere, we will work with you to determine how to pull those into the BCDevExchange. Contact us for more information.
